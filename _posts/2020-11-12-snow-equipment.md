<p>Review the type and quantity of equipment used during winter weather events.</p><p>This dataset is used on an annual basis to plan for snow and ice events during the winter months.  It provides the equivalents of each equipment type the Town of Cary uses to plow, spread and brine roads before and during winter weather events.  </p><p>The variance in truck and plow size is normalized using Spreader and Plow equivalent values.</p>
